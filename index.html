Dino Sombrero

Instructions

  1. You begin with 3 lives
  2. Meteors, hats and tacos come from right to left
  3. You gain 1 point when you collect a hat
  4. You gain a life when you eat a tacos
  5. You lose a life when you get hit by a meteor
  6. You win the game when you get to 50 points
  7. It's gameOver when you run out of lives
  8. Have fun!
